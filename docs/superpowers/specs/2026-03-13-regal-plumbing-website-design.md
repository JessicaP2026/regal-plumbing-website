## 8. SEO Strategy

### On-Page SEO
- Each page targets primary keywords (e.g., "plumber in Rancho Cucamonga", "24/7 emergency plumber Inland Empire")
- Each service card/page targets service-specific keywords (e.g., "drain cleaning Pomona", "slab leak repair Ontario CA")
- City pages (future) target "[service] in [city]" long-tail keywords
- Draft title tags and meta descriptions to be approved by client before implementation (see Section 10)

### Schema Markup (via RankMath)
| Page | Schema Types |
|------|-------------|
| All pages | LocalBusiness, Plumber (areaServed: 32 cities, telephone, openingHours: 24/7) |
| Services Overview | Service (each of the 8 services) |
| About Us | Organization |
| Contact Us | ContactPage |
| Service Area | LocalBusiness with areaServed expanded |

### Technical SEO
- Fast-loading Elementor build (optimized images, caching plugin)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Clean URL structure: /services/, /about/, /contact/, /service-area/
- Future service pages: /services/drain-cleaning/, /services/emergency-plumbing/, etc.
- XML sitemap + Google Search Console integration
- RankMath SEO plugin

### Local SEO
- NAP consistency across all pages: Regal Plumbing & Rooter | 2141 E Philadelphia St, Suite R, Ontario, CA 91761 | (909) 600-4561
- Google Business Profile integration (active profile confirmed)
- Google Maps iframe embed (standard embed, no API key required) on Contact and Service Area pages
- City-specific landing pages (future expansion — city links in Service Area grid point to /service-area/[city-slug]/ as placeholder pages at launch)

## 9. Conversion Strategy

- Sticky header with always-visible phone number
- Top emergency bar on every page
- Red CTA banner section on every page
- CTAs appear every 2–3 sections on homepage
- "Get a Free Quote" CTA on homepage hero links to Contact Us page, anchored directly to the contact form
- Contact form with city + service dropdowns for qualified lead capture
- Contact form submits to client-specified email address via WP Mail SMTP
- Contact form includes reCAPTCHA spam protection
- Form submission shows inline success message and redirects to /thank-you/ page (for future conversion tracking)
- Trust signals (license, family-owned, 24/7) repeated throughout
- Google Reviews section on homepage — live Google Reviews plugin (requires Google Business Profile); fallback: 3 manually curated real review cards at launch
- **Mobile:** Persistent "Call Now" sticky bar at bottom of screen on mobile — highest-converting pattern for service business mobile visitors
- Mobile header collapses to hamburger menu; top emergency bar remains visible

### Services Overview "Learn More" Buttons — Phase 1 Behavior
Individual service detail pages are out of scope for Phase 1. At launch, each "Learn More" button on the Services Overview page links to the Contact Us page with the relevant service pre-selected in the Service Needed dropdown via URL parameter (e.g., `/contact/?service=drain-cleaning`). The WPForms field must be configured to read this `?service=` parameter and pre-populate the Service Needed dropdown accordingly. Accepted values match the service slugs: `emergency-plumbing`, `drain-cleaning`, `sewer-line-repair`, `water-heater`, `slab-leak`, `hydrojetting`, `gas-leak-detection`, `water-filtration`.
